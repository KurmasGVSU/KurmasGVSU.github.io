Heath Insurance

Sponsor

Zack Kurmas
kurmasz@cc.gatech.edu
Area Systems

Problem
The health insurance available to students at Georgia Tech sucks. Furthermore, only the Board of Regents, or the state legislature has the power to fix it.

Deliverable
A bill requiring the University System of Georgia to provide all GRAs and GTAs the same level of coverage offered to full-time faculty at the same price.

Evaluation
You will receive 1 point for every member of the General Assembly who votes for your bill. You will receive full credit if the bill is signed into law. No credit will be given if the bill is never brought up for a vote.
