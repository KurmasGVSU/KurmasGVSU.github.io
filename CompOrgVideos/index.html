CIS 351 Supplementary Videos

Below is a series of videos I use in CIS 351, Grand Valley State University's course on Computer Organization and Assembly Language.

  • This playlist contains all the videos below.
  • These videos introduce topics in a "just-in-time" manner: I don't introduce topics a topic until it is needed to solve a larger problem. For example, I don't introduce two's complement until after we have covered adders and are ready to introduce the addition of negative numbers and subtraction. Similarly, I don't introduce multiplexers until they are needed for the carry select adder and ALUs.
  • The pace of the videos is a bit slow (I tend to stumble over my words if I try to talk faster). I recommend watching them at 1.25x speed.
  • Feel free to use these videos in your course. If you do, please encourage all your students to subscribe to the channel.
