Text:
The official textbook for this class is
  • Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces by Remzi and Andrea Arpaci-Dusseau. ISBN: 198508659X https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~remzi/OSTEP/

This book is available for free online. You can also order a print copy for a reasonable prince. If you do, take care. The authors update the online copy periodically. Make sure any print copy you order is not out of date.

You may also find this text helpful:

  • Operating System Concepts Essentials by Silberschatz, Galvin, and Gagne. ISBN: 0470889209

It is available through the GVSU library.
